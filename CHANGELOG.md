# Changelog

What's new on Signal Tracker — new features, improvements, and fixes. Entries are
newest first. Spotted something wrong? Every call has a "Report incorrect" button.

## 2026-06-25

### Improved
- **Search creators and stocks from the sidebar** — The sidebar's Creators and Stocks lists are now searchable across the entire database, not just the names shown. Type a handle, name, or ticker to jump straight to anyone tracked.

## 2026-06-24

### Improved
- **Smoother creator and stock switching** — Switching between creators or tickers on a stock page now animates in place instead of redrawing abruptly.
- **Compare creators side by side** — The stock page now lines up each creator's calls on one grid, so you can see who called a ticker and how those calls did against each other.
- **Proof on the Explore page** — Tap any row on Explore to open the original post — reel or tweet — right there, the same way you already can on a stock page. You can also paste a post's link into search to jump straight to its call.
- **Stats that follow you** — A creator's hit rate and average return now stay pinned in the header as you scroll past the top of their page.
- **Circle-blur theme switch** — Flipping between light and dark now sweeps across the screen with a soft blur instead of snapping.

### Fixed
- **See other tickers from the same post** — When a post pitches several stocks, the proof view now links the sibling tickers so you can hop between them.
- **Sort arrows on the leaderboard** — Sortable columns now show which way they're sorted.
- **Single-caller stock pages** — A ticker only one creator has called now opens straight to that creator's view instead of an empty "all creators" mode.

## 2026-06-22

### Improved
- **Your movers in the sidebar** — The most recently called tickers now sit in the sidebar with a live one-day mini-chart, so the day's movers are one glance away from anywhere on the site.

### Fixed
- **Smoother chart loading** — Charts now fade in behind a matching placeholder instead of popping into place.
- **Steadier first paint** — Removed a brief flash when a chart first appears on screen.

## 2026-06-19

### Improved
- **Performance over time** — Each creator page now opens with a running line of how their picks have done versus the S&P 500 — the whole trend at a glance, not just the latest number.

## 2026-06-18

> See whether a stock is Shariah-compliant, and follow any ticker across creators.

### New
- **Halal compliance badges** — Switch on Halal status in preferences to see Shariah-compliance ratings on creators and stocks, with a breakdown of how clean each company's revenue and balance sheet are. Ratings come from Musaffa.
- **A page for every stock** — Open any ticker to see every creator who called it, switch between them, and read their calls on a single timeline.

## 2026-06-15

### New
- **Several stocks in one post** — When a creator pitches more than one ticker in a post ("loading up on NVDA and AMD"), each pick is now tracked and scored on its own, instead of only the first one.

### Improved
- **Better link previews** — Sharing a creator or stock link now unfurls with a custom preview image showing the price chart.

## 2026-06-14

> Spot a call we got wrong? Now you can flag it.

### New
- **Report incorrect calls** — Every call has a "Report incorrect" button. Flag a wrong ticker, direction, or a post that wasn't really a call — reports feed a review queue and confirmed corrections are folded into the scores.

## 2026-06-13

### New
- **Crypto picks are scored too** — Bitcoin, Ethereum, and other crypto calls are now tracked and scored alongside stocks.
- **Clearer price charts** — Ticker charts gained a live headline price, percentage change, dollar-axis labels, and smooth transitions when you switch timeframes.

### Improved
- **Only real picks count** — Calls to "buy the market" (index funds and ETFs) are no longer counted as individual stock picks, so accuracy reflects actual stock-picking.

## 2026-06-10

> Explore every call from every creator in one place.

### New
- **Explore all calls** — A new Explore page to filter, sort, and search every scored call across all tracked creators.
- **Per-stock pages** — See all the calls on a single stock, across every creator, in one view.

## 2026-06-06

### New
- **Preferences** — Choose a theme, reduce motion, and turn optional features on or off from a new preferences panel. On mobile, the charts and controls now give subtle haptic feedback.

## 2026-06-04

### New
- **"How to read this" guide** — A short explainer on the home page covering what the numbers mean and how calls are scored.

## 2026-06-03

> Interactive charts, proof behind every call, and a creator leaderboard.

### New
- **Live, interactive charts** — Zoomable price charts with selectable timeframes from one month to all-time, with each call marked on the timeline where it was made.
- **Proof behind every call** — Tap any call to see the original post — Instagram reel or tweet — embedded, alongside the quote and a plain-language summary.
- **Creator leaderboard** — Sort creators by hit rate and returns, with sample sizes and a low-confidence flag for thin track records.
- **Per-call mini-charts** — A small sparkline on each call showing how the stock moved since the post.

## 2026-06-02

> Signal Tracker is live.

### New
- **Track finfluencer stock calls** — Signal Tracker scores stock picks from Instagram and X/Twitter creators by their forward returns versus the S&P 500 — measured at one week, one month, three months, and to date.
